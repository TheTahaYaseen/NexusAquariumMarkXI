Story of the Beginnings of this Aquarium

In the Late 1980's 2 Brothers known by the name of Wilson Brothers had a Small Shop in the city of Washington DC. This was your ordinary fish shop where you would find goldfishes, guppies etc. but they had one Special Clownfish which they named Nexus (hence the name of the aquarium) which had been living with them for 3 years, so, when they were being kicked by their landlord, the fish tank in which Nexus was Fell down but the Landlord's Guards wouldn't let them help the poor fish and theye watched him dying. It was then they decided that they would establish such a Business so they never have to feel this Embarrased and Woudn't have to see a Family Member (They considered Nexus as thir family as they had no real one) Die becuse of them .

Teen Consevation Leaders (TCL)

The Teen Conservation Leaders (TCL) program provides an important volunteer service to the Aquarium, our guests and our communities.During a fun and intensive two-week training at the beginning of the summer, students learn basic marine biology and ocean conservation.

Upon completion of the training, students provide exhibit interpretation for our guests. As a Teen Conservation Leader, students develop important job and life skills, build self-confidence and earn community service hours.
