Animals

The Nexus Aquarium in Washington is home to many sea animals consisting of more than 70 Species which are contained in a safe and sanitary enviorment. There is a great variety of animals present in our Aquarium but Some of the Exotic Animals our Aquarium has are African Penguins, Beluga Whales, Manta Ray, Whale Shark, Loogerhead Sea Turtle, Chrysaora, Axolotls (An Amphibian you may not heard of) and many more.
